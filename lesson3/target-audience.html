Target Audience Description

The target audience would be those who do more outdoor activities. The age range of the audience is mostly around 18-55 who go out more. For instance, attend a school or go to work. Also, they are more likely to be familiar with the internet. The website would suit the needs of the audience by its convenience and the easiness of getting accurate information.
